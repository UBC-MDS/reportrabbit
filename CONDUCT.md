# Code of Conduct: DSCI 524: Group 11

# Introduction  
Our team is dedicated to fostering a collaborative, inclusive, and respectful environment where ideas can be shared openly and learning is encouraged. All participants, whether contributing through discussions, code, documentation, or reviews are expected to support a positive and constructive atmosphere.

This Code of Conduct applies to all project-related interactions.

# Commitment to Diversity and Inclusion  
Our project is open to people of all backgrounds, regardless of age, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, ethnicity, nationality, race, religion, education, or any other personal characteristic. We are committed to creating an environment where everyone feels respected, valued, and comfortable contributing without fear of discrimination or exclusion.

# Expected Behaviour  
All members of this project community are expected to act with professionalism, integrity, and openness. This includes:
- Communicating with respect and consideration at all times
- Being open to othersâ€™ perspectives and acknowledging their contributions
- Offering and accepting feedback in a constructive and professional way
- Participating in discussions that encourage teamwork and shared understanding

# Inappropriate Behavior  
- Any form of harassment toward participants
- Intentional intimidation, stalking, or persistent following
- Threatening or violent language directed at others
- Sharing or displaying offensive, explicit, or inappropriate content
- Use of slurs, excessive profanity, or aggressive and hostile language

All members are expected to refrain from all forms of unacceptable conduct to maintain a respectful environment.

# Consequences for Unacceptable Behavior  
If a participant engages in inappropriate behavior, they will be asked to stop and comply immediately. If the behavior persists or the issue is severe, it may be referred to the teaching team or course faculty. Ongoing violations may lead to removal from the project.

# Reporting Concerns and Guidelines  
If a participant violates this Code of Conduct, the incident may be reported by contacting Raghav Gupta at raghav90@student.ubc.ca. All reports will be treated seriously and handled with discretion. The Group 11 team will review the situation and take actions it considers appropriate given the circumstances.
